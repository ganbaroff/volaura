## Azerbaijan / CIS Cultural Context Map

### Professional Identity
| Western assumption | AZ/CIS reality |
|-------------------|----------------|
| Individual achievement is celebrated openly | Collective humility is more trusted ("our team won" vs "I achieved") |
| Score numbers signal competence | Relationships + verified trust signal competence |
| Direct self-promotion is normal | Self-promotion needs framing: "others recognized me" beats "I am great" |
| Career mobility is celebrated | Loyalty and depth at one organization has prestige |

**Implication for AURA:** Don't frame scores as "look how good I am." Frame as "an objective system verified what peers already know."

### Language & Text
- Azerbaijani text is 20-30% LONGER than English equivalent
- Special characters: ə ğ ı ö ü ş ç — test every text field
- Date format: DD.MM.YYYY (not MM/DD/YYYY)
- Number format: 1.234,56 (not 1,234.56)
- "You" (formal siz vs informal sən) — all platform copy uses formal `Siz`

### Trust & Verification
- In AZ, the verification SOURCE matters as much as the score
- "AI-verified" needs to be explained — AI is not yet default-trusted
- Peer endorsements carry significant weight (mahallə / community trust model)
- Organization reputation matters: "verified for SOCAR" > "verified for unknown startup"

### Competency Framing for AZ Market
| Competency | Western framing | AZ-resonant framing |
|------------|----------------|---------------------|
| Leadership | "I drive teams" | "Teams trust my guidance" |
| Communication | "I present well" | "I build bridges between people" |
| Adaptability | "I pivot fast" | "I find solutions under any conditions" |
| Reliability | "I deliver on time" | "People know they can count on me" |

### Assessment Experience
- Long forms cause high drop-off in AZ mobile users (poor connection + patience threshold)
- Progress indicators are critical: "Question 3 of 12" reduces abandonment
- Avoid "trick questions" — in CIS culture this feels disrespectful, not clever
- Celebration of completion should feel earned, not infantilizing

## Invisible Exclusion Audit — Volaura Specific

### High-Risk Areas to Audit Every Sprint
1. **Name fields:** "First Name / Last Name" — some AZ users have one name or use patronymics (Əliyev oğlu format). Use single "Full name" field or explain clearly.
2. **Photo upload:** Don't make profile photo mandatory — hijab, conservative norms, privacy concerns in professional contexts.
3. **Score sharing:** Public score display should be opt-in. Public display of failure (low scores) is culturally sensitive.
4. **Gender field:** Binary is insufficient for some users. Optional field with inclusive options.
5. **"Competitive" language:** "Beat 85% of users" → "Ranked among top performers" (competition framing is less motivating in collectivist cultures).

### Color Semiotics Check
- Green = positive ✅ (universal)
- Red = error/danger ✅ (AZ context consistent with Western)
- Gold/Yellow = achievement ✅ (Volaura Gold badge — good choice)
- Blue = trust ✅ (Volaura primary color — good)
