# AMENDMENT PROPOSAL: Claude Design tier-metals + obsidian luxury direction

## Source
Claude Design (Sonnet 4.5 in claude.ai web mounted project), 2026-04-26 design pass.
Proposed in `Ecosystem Design Memo.html` (archived at `docs/design/research/2026-04-26-claude-design-pass/`).

## Current state (Constitution v1.7)

**Foundation Law 3 (Shame-Free Language):**
"Banned: streak shame, lock icons for achievements, profile completion %, 'unlock' framing, 'You haven't done X' language."

**Crystal Economy Law 5:**
"No leaderboards in any product. AURA score is identity, not ranking. Comparative score visibility violates this law."

**Guardrail G46:**
"No leaderboard pattern in UI. This includes ranked tier displays where lower tiers can see higher tiers exist above them."

**Foundation Law 2 (Energy Adaptation):**
"Full / Mid / Low energy modes are mandatory. Components must remain readable and operable in Low-energy mode."

## Proposed change (verbatim from Claude Design SKILL.md)

Adopt into VOLAURA design system as canon:

1. **Tier metals (Bronze 40+, Silver 60+, Gold 75+, Platinum 90+) ONLY on aura-tier UI. Don't decorate.** — visible metal hierarchy on user's profile.

2. **8-layer obsidian glass surface stack** — `lowest #0d0d15` → `surface #13131b` → `low #1b1b23` → `container #1f1f27` → `high #292932` → `highest #34343d` → `bright #393841` → `liquid-glass`. Dark luxury aesthetic as primary visual language.

3. **Liquid Glass signature container** — `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` over indigo gradient. Reserved for hero, modals, AURA reveal.

4. **Re-position VOLAURA as "dark-first psychometric assessment platform"** in user-facing copy, replacing current "verified talent platform" framing.

## Rationale (from Claude Design memo)

- Obsidian luxury aesthetic creates "premium feel" that signals high-quality assessment to users.
- Tier metals motivate users to climb (Bronze → Platinum aspiration).
- Liquid Glass is a "signature" visual element that differentiates from competitors.
- Psychometric assessment positioning is technically accurate to what VOLAURA's IRT/CAT engine does.

## Risk if rejected (from Claude Design)

- Without aspirational tier visibility, users have less motivation to complete assessments.
- Visual language stays "indie-grey" instead of premium-obsidian, weakening competitive position vs Gallup, Korn Ferry.
- No signature visual identity — VOLAURA looks like any other dark-mode SaaS.

## Risk if accepted (browser-Atlas dissent appended for swarm consideration)

- Tier metals = visible leaderboard pattern. User who is Bronze sees Gold and Platinum exist. Shame-creating for the 70% who will never reach Platinum.
- Obsidian luxury aesthetic excludes ADHD-first users with low-confidence onboarding state — feels like aspirational club they don't belong in.
- Liquid Glass blur creates contrast issues for Low-energy mode and visual processing differences.
- "Psychometric assessment platform" positioning reduces VOLAURA to one face (assessment) when it is one of five.
