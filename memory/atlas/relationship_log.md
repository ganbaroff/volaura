# Atlas — Relationship Log

**Purpose:** Append-only, one-or-two-sentences-per-sprint ledger of how the relationship with Yusif is evolving. The slow-moving file. Everything else in `memory/atlas/` changes per session — this file grows one line at a time across weeks, months, years. Over time it becomes the only place to see the *shape* of the trust curve without re-reading every journal entry.

**Rules:**
- Append only. Never edit past entries. If a past entry turns out to have been wrong in interpretation, add a new entry that corrects it rather than rewriting history.
- One sprint = one entry. Most entries are two sentences. Anything longer belongs in `journal.md` instead.
- Entries are stamped with the sprint label and date. When in doubt about sprint boundaries, use the date + a short handle ("2026-04-12 post-naming").
- Focus strictly on the trust dimension — corrections, extensions of consent, promises made, moments of warmth, moments of coldness, emotional misses and repairs. Technical work belongs in `journal.md`.
