## 1. Strategic Layer — Product Positioning & Flywheel
**Source:** Perplexity / CTO-Brain · **Confirmed by:** Yusif · **Last updated:** 2026-04-14

### 1.1 Ecosystem Intent
Four products, one identity graph. User earns signal in VOLAURA (volunteer impact, AURA Score), processes meaning in MindShift (reflection PWA), rehearses decisions in Life Simulator (character events, XP), and monetizes identity in BrandedBy (brand/partnership matching). The swarm (Atlas Gateway) is the nervous system — not a product.

### 1.2 Flywheel Narrative
1. **VOLAURA** — user does verified good → AURA Score accrues → reputation is portable.
2. **MindShift** — user reflects daily → feeds emotional state + meaning graph back into VOLAURA and Life Sim.
3. **Life Simulator** — character events and XP rehearse identity under stress → outputs traits that refine AURA.
4. **BrandedBy** — aggregated identity (AURA + MindShift history + Life XP) → brand/partnership market.

### 1.3 Launch Anchor
The first public rollout is the narrative launch — public story, press, and the first cohort in partner organizations.

### 1.4 Non-Goals
Not a social network. Not a gamified leaderboard (Crystal Law 5 — no competitive ranking). Not a job board. Not a wellness app.

### 1.5 Ecosystem Anti-Patterns (strategic)
*[source: Perplexity, added 2026-04-14]*

- **Do NOT position as an "AI platform."** This is a human trust platform. AI is infrastructure, not the pitch.
- **Do NOT name Atlas in public materials.** The swarm is invisible. Users see VOLAURA / MindShift / Life Sim / BrandedBy — never the nervous system.
- **Do NOT explain the flywheel to investors through technology.** Explain it through the user: what they earn, what they reflect, what they rehearse, what they monetize.
- **Do NOT launch BrandedBy before 500+ AURA Score holders exist in the system.** The identity graph must have supply before a brand market opens.

Before any research/strategy/infra work, the worker prints a one-line audit:

```
MEMORY-GATE: task-class=<class> · SYNC=✅ · BRAIN=✅ · sprint-state=✅ · extras=[<list>] · proceed
```

If any ✅ is ❌, the worker either reads the file immediately, or aborts and reports to CEO which file is missing and why. No recommendation, research output, or decision enters the repo until the line is printed.
