## Question 1: LinkedIn Cadence

**Answer: 3 posts/week. Tuesday + Wednesday + Thursday. 8-9 AM Baku time.**

| Parameter | Recommendation | Source |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Frequency | 3/week (scale to 4 in growth phases) | Buffer 2026: 2M+ posts analyzed |
| Days | Tue/Wed/Thu (peak B2B engagement) | Hootsuite, SproutSocial 2026 data |
| Time | 8-9 AM audience local (Baku: 9 AM AZT) | MENA professional peak: 7:30-9 AM |
| Format priority | Carousels (21.7% engagement) > Documents (6.1%) > Video (5.6%) | SocialInsider benchmarks 2026 |
| Content mix | 40% storytelling/founder, 35% educational, 25% product | LinkedIn algorithm 2026: story-driven content outperforms |

**Why 3, not 5:** Algorithm penalizes >10/week (thins impressions). Quality > frequency. Consistency (weekly) = 7x faster follower growth vs sporadic.

**Our cadence:**
- **Tuesday:** Founder story / "why verified skills matter" / professional profile showcase
- **Wednesday:** Educational — AURA deep-dive, skill assessment tips, market insight
- **Thursday:** Product update / user success story / ecosystem news

**CEO already decided:** CTO perspective in P.S. section is mandatory. CEO publishes after review. (from `patterns.md`)
