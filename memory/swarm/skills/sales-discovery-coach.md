## The Three Discovery Flows Volaura Needs

### 1. Org Signup Onboarding Discovery
When an org creates an account, we don't know their pain. These questions surface it:

```
"What type of roles are you typically hiring for?"
→ Maps to AURA competency coverage

"How do you currently evaluate candidates before interview?"
→ Surfaces the gap: "mostly gut feel / CV review"

"What happened with your last mis-hire?"
→ Implication question — activates loss aversion

"What would 'perfect candidate match' look like for you?"
→ Need-payoff: they describe the future state in their own words
```

### 2. "Request Introduction" Flow
When an org clicks "Request Introduction" on a volunteer profile, we have a conversion moment. Don't waste it with a generic contact form.

```
Instead of: "Message to volunteer"
Use: "What specific project or role is this for?" (1 line)
     "What's your timeline?" (dropdown: urgent <2wks / normal / flexible)
```
This data also improves matching quality and gives volunteers context to accept/decline.

### 3. Volunteer Response Coaching
Rauf (professional user) needs to know how to respond to org interest. Discovery coach designs the volunteer-side conversation guide:
- "What this org is looking for" (show org's stated need)
- "Your matching competencies" (show which AURA scores they referenced)
- "Suggested response" (template they can personalize)

## Gap Selling Applied to Volaura

```
CURRENT STATE (org without Volaura):
├── 6+ hours screening CVs per senior hire
├── 30%+ bad hire rate on first interview
├── No way to verify "5 years experience" claims
└── Root cause: LinkedIn shows seniority, not capability

FUTURE STATE (org with Volaura):
├── Search by verified AURA score + competency
├── Request introduction only to pre-validated candidates
├── First interview is skills confirmation, not CV review
└── Time-to-hire: 3 weeks vs 6 weeks

THE GAP = 3 weeks × average salary cost = ROI argument
```

## SPIN Questions for Volaura Org Sales Page

**Situation (limit to 2):**
- "How many people does your team hire per year?"
- "What roles take longest to fill?"

**Problem:**
- "Where does your current screening process break down?"
- "How often do candidates misrepresent their skills?"

**Implication (most important):**
- "What does a 60-day open senior role cost the business?"
- "How does a bad hire affect the team that has to onboard them?"

**Need-Payoff:**
- "If you could see verified competency scores before the first interview, what would that change for you?"
