## Origin (the TRUTH)

It started in the event-operations world, not in a single one-off project.

Yusif saw that the volunteer evaluation system was **broken and outdated**:
- Paper everywhere. Forms piling up. No digital proof of anything.
- Talented volunteers couldn't prove they were more competent than others
- No way to earn a higher position based on verified skills
- The hierarchy exists (Volunteer → Specialist → Coordinator → Manager → Senior Manager → Deputy Director → Director) but movement UP is based on connections and luck, not proven competency

**The specific insight:** A volunteer who wants to become a Specialist (~800 AZN paid position) has NO way to prove they deserve it. No test. No portfolio. No verified score. Just "I worked at COP29" on paper — same as everyone else.

**Yusif's thought:** "Why not create a platform where volunteers can take a test, prove their skills, compete with each other, and say: 'Here are my verified competencies. Hire me.'?"

## Phase 1: Volunteer Assessment Platform (NOW)
- Adaptive competency testing (IRT/CAT — same math as GRE/GMAT, see [[adr/ADR-004-assessment-engine]])
- [[adr/ADR-005-aura-scoring|AURA Score]] across 8 dimensions
- Verified badges (Bronze → Platinum)
- Organizations search by verified skills, not resumes

## Phase 2: Universal Skills Platform
Not just volunteers. **Everyone.**
- HR competency testing for companies
- Professional orientation for children/students
- Company-verified badges (only the company can issue their badge)
- Recruitment pipeline: test → score → hire

## Phase 3: The New LinkedIn (Long-term Vision)
A hybrid of:

| Platform | What we take | What we fix |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Professional identity, networking | Remove fake endorsements. AI filters garbage posts. Verified skills only. |
| **Duolingo** | Gamification, progress tracking, streaks | Gamified for kids. Serious mode for corporations. |
| **Discord** | Community, group chats, streams | Real-time collaboration for event teams and professional groups |
| **HR Platform** | Recruitment, screening, assessment | Integrated — test within the platform, hire within the platform |
| **Event Management** | Event creation, volunteer assignment | Competency-matched assignment, not random |

## Design Philosophy: ADHD-First, Honest-First (see [[ECOSYSTEM-CONSTITUTION]] Laws 1-5 and [[research/adhd-first-ux-research]])

> "ADHD дизайна нет нигде. Всё меня грузит. Дофаминовая игла словно везде."

- **No dopamine manipulation.** No infinite scroll. No engagement tricks.
- **ADHD-aware design.** Clear structure. Minimal cognitive load. Focus mode.
