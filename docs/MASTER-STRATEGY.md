## Volaura Flywheel Business Model

```
Volunteers join (free, for AURA badge + opportunities)
  → MiroFish tests them (data grows)
    → Organizations pay for verified talent pool (revenue)
      → Revenue attracts City Leads in new cities (growth)
        → More volunteers → more data → more organizations
          → Data moat = competitive advantage
            → Grants + investment = scale
              → REPEAT
```

### Revenue Model (calibrated for Azerbaijan, 2026-03-24 — see also [[MONETIZATION-ROADMAP]] and [[research/geo-pricing-research]])

**Market data:** Humanique.az = 10-15% annual salary per hire (~$500-1,400).
Boss.az = 20-50 AZN ($12-30) per posting. Median Baku income: 310 AZN ($185).

#### Subscriptions (AZN — local currency pricing)

| Tier | AZN/mo | USD/mo | Target | Volunteers |
|------|--------|--------|--------|------------|
| Free | 0 | $0 | Individual volunteers, tiny NGOs | ≤10 |
| Starter | 49 | ~$29 | Small NGOs, event orgs | ≤25 |
| Professional | 129 | ~$79 | Event companies, medium orgs | ≤100 |
| Business | 339 | ~$199 | Corporations, large events | ≤500 |
| Enterprise | 849+ | ~$499+ | Oil/gas, government, UN | Unlimited + SLA |

**Why:** Boss.az = 20-50 AZN. Starter at 49 AZN feels natural for local market.
Enterprise (849 AZN) = only ~5-10 orgs in Azerbaijan can afford this.
**Volume comes from Starter ($29) and Professional ($79).**

#### Per-Verified-Placement Fee (add-on, not default)

| Level | AZN | USD | vs Humanique |
|-------|-----|-----|-------------|
| Junior | 250 | ~$150 | 60-75% cheaper |
| Specialist | 425 | ~$250 | 60-75% cheaper |
| Senior/Lead | 680 | ~$400 | 60-75% cheaper |

**Key:** NOT "pay per volunteer" — pay per VERIFIED PLACEMENT with competency guarantee.
Free volunteers stay free. Fee is for matching + verification service.

#### AI Assessment API (B2B — Pasha Bank model)

| Volume | AZN/test | USD/test |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 1-100/mo | 8.50 | ~$5 |
| 100-500/mo | 5 | ~$3 |
| 500+/mo | 3.40 | ~$2 |
