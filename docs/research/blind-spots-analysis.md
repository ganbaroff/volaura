## 10 Critical Findings (severity-ranked)

### 1. TAM is 10-15x Smaller Than Assumed (9/10)
AZ = 10.3M people, but addressable = 500-700K (Baku, 20-35, digitally engaged).
**Action:** Define TAM as "verified profiles + hiring orgs in AZ + diaspora UAE/RU/TR."

### 2. BrandedBy is Legally Indefensible Today (9/10)
NO FAKES Act (April 2025), TAKE IT DOWN Act (May 2025), EU AI Act high-risk.
SIMA verification ≠ likeness licensing rights. Agency contracts override.
**Action:** IP attorney opinion (US/UK) BEFORE any commercial celebrity video.

### 3. 5 Products = Maintenance Debt Bomb (8/10)
Next.js migration, Supabase API changes, OWASP patches — all multiply by 5.
**Action:** Volaura = primary for 12 months. Others = R&D prototypes only.

### 4. Cross-Product Data Sharing Violates Privacy Law (8/10)
GDPR Article 5(1)(b) purpose limitation. LinkedIn fined EUR310M (Oct 2024).
Game behavior → hiring decisions = textbook GDPR Article 22 violation.
**Action:** Data Processing Agreement per cross-product data flow BEFORE connecting.

### 5. Three Simultaneous Chicken-and-Egg Problems (8/10)
Volaura needs orgs + volunteers. Life Sim needs verified users. BrandedBy needs celebrities + brands.
**Action:** Sequence: Volaura supply side (3-5 NGO partnerships) → 500 profiles → demand side → rest.

### 6. LTV/CAC Math Broken at Current Pricing (8/10)
48% churn in 6 months → avg subscriber stays 2.1 months → $6.20 revenue vs $15 CAC.
**Action:** B2B subscription (org access AZN 50-200/mo) more defensible than B2C.

### 7. "LinkedIn Killer" Framing Gets You Killed (7/10)
LinkedIn has 1B users, $15B revenue, Microsoft backing. Can replicate verified skills in 6 months.
**Action:** Rebrand internally: "verified competency hiring for volunteer-sector and civic orgs in AZ."

### 8. Crystal Economy Has No Monetary Policy (7/10)
No sinks, no inflation control, no exchange rate governance.
**Action:** 1-page monetary policy: supply cap, sinks list, daily earn cap, no direct purchase.

### 9. Gamified Retention Requires Resources You Don't Have (7/10)
52% drop off within 30 days. Content refresh, A/B testing, clinical validation all need team.
**Action:** Procedural content generation (IRT engine). MindShift: "patterns" not "burnout detection."

### 10. Swarm = Single Point of Failure (6/10)
One API key, one Railway, one Supabase, one human reviewer.
**Action:** 1-page system runbook: credentials access, minimum maintenance per product, pause states.

## The Verdict
Technically impressive ecosystem. The ecosystem architecture IS the problem — distributes
scarce founder capacity across 5 products in a market that can't yet absorb 1 profitably.
**Right move:** Volaura only for 12 months. Everything else = prototype. Prove LTV/CAC. Then expand.
