## Adversary failure modes (the value of strange-protocol — it broke my first answer)
- **FM1 — Accelerator-as-PRIMARY is a lottery, not a plan.** YC accept ~1.5–2%, lower for a solo, non-engineer-by-trade, PRE-USER founder from a non-hub. Building a 6–9-month plan on it collapses if rejected (likely). *Signal:* 6–8 weeks sunk on the app, rejected/waitlisted by Q4, nothing else to show.
- **FM2 — "Runway is fine / no raise" hides the real bottlenecks, which need CASH not credits.** AZ data-consent legal clearance (~$2–5k local counsel, Law on Personal Data), B2B sales bandwidth (solo can't run product+sales+eng past 2 pilots), pilot procurement/DPA paperwork. GITA grants are slow (3–6mo) + restricted to eng hires. *Signal:* legal takes 4–6mo not 6–8wk, first pilot stalls on DPA, founder bottlenecked on 3 workstreams.
- **FM3 — The "event-ops pilot wedge" validates the WRONG buyer and dilutes positioning.** Event orgs hire temp/volunteer staff (300–600 AZN/engagement), won't pay per-hire SaaS; their budget holder is an ops director, not HR. Using that network as the CUSTOMER validates a weaker business and risks reframing VOLAURA as an "event-volunteer tool," diluting the COP29 credential. *Signal:* pilot used for volunteer intake, "traction" slide doesn't match the talent-platform ICP, investors ask why an event case validates the thesis.

## DECISION — "paid proof first, accelerator second"
1. **PRIMARY (next 90 days):** land **2 PAID token-price ($500–1k) pilots with CORPORATE HR buyers** reached through Yusif's COP29/WUF13 network — i.e. the state enterprises / international organizations that STAFFED those events, **not event-ops orgs as the customer**. Deliverable per pilot: "run ~10 external candidates through AURA; tell us whether the score predicted hire quality better than your CV screen." Goal = a paying logo + a predictive-validity data point, with the RIGHT buyer persona (positioning stays clean).
2. **UNBLOCK (cash, now):** ~**$3k local counsel** to clear AZ data-protection consent so pilots run on real external candidates. This is the true blocker; credits don't touch it. Scope pilots to **AZ first** (EU later).
3. **PARALLEL (non-dilutive):** GITA grant for engineering help — slow + restricted, so a parallel track, NOT the critical path.
4. **DECK FRAME:** NOT "raising $X." Ask slide = *"Seeking 2 design-partner employers + a data-consent legal clear — to turn a live assessment engine into proven hiring outcomes."* Accelerator (YC S27 / Techstars) + Series A $500K–1M = **future signals**, applied-to WITH traction, not from zero.
