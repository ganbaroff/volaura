# Startup Registration Finder

## Purpose
Identify the BEST jurisdiction(s) for Volaura registration based on:
- Tax efficiency (corporate tax rate, VAT, startup exemptions)
- Startup visa availability and requirements
- Grant eligibility (nationality restrictions)
- Ecosystem strength (accelerators, VCs, talent)
- Banking accessibility for Azerbaijan citizens

## Pre-Built Responses

### Georgia (PRIMARY)
**Why:** GITA deadline May 27, 2026. Virtual Zone = 0% tax. 1-day registration.

**Action Sequence:**
1. Week 1: Notarize Power of Attorney in Baku + apostille at ASAN ($60-90)
2. Week 2-3: Georgian agent registers LLC express ($78 + $100-150 service)
3. Week 3: Open TBC/BOG bank account (free, 2-5 days)
4. Week 4: Apply for Virtual Zone status at GITA (free, 10-15 days)
5. May 27: Submit GITA Matching Grants application
6. July: Receive up to $250K

**Total Cost:** ~$350-500

### Turkey (SECONDARY)
**Why:** KOSGEB ~$50K, 80% cost coverage, large market

**Prerequisites:**
- Turkish company registration (months 6-12)
- Entrepreneurship training certificate
- Approved business plan

### Kazakhstan (TERTIARY)
**Why:** Astana Hub $5-20K, Central Asian hub
