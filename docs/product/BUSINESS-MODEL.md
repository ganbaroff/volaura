# Volaura Business Model

## Overview
Volaura monetizes a verified talent platform by serving two primary user segments: **individual users** and **organizations** seeking trusted talent. The platform creates value through AURA Score credibility, reducing friction in discovery, recruitment, and matching.

**Launch Geography:** Azerbaijan (primary), with CIS/MENA expansion roadmap.
**Target Scale:** 200+ profiles at first launch, 10,000+ within 6 months.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Freemium Individual Tier (Free)
**Target User:** First-time user exploring the platform.

**Features:**
- Complete AURA assessment (5–8 competencies via CAT/IRT)
- AURA score + badge (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- Public profile with assessed history, skills, and endorsements
- Basic event discovery and registration
- Share AURA score card (social media, WhatsApp)
- Reassessment every 90 days

**Rationale:**
- Zero friction entry maximizes adoption and viral potential
- Public profiles create network effects (orgs see volunteer talent)
- Genuine utility prevents users from seeking competitors
- Drives KPIs and launch event metrics

---

### 2. Pro Volunteer Tier ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)
**Target User:** Active volunteer seeking professional credentialing and optimization.

**Features:**
- **Skill DNA visualization:** Radar chart of competency strengths/gaps with peer benchmarks
- **Advanced share cards:** Custom backgrounds, LinkedIn carousel, QR code integration
- **Priority event matching:** AI-powered event recommendations based on AURA profile
- **Accelerated reassessment:** Every 30 days (vs 90 days free tier)
- **Certificate PDF:** Download and share verified certificate with tamper-proof QR code
- **Detailed analytics:** Score trends over time, percentile vs peer group, competency growth trajectory
- **Leaderboard ranking:** Competitive standing, badge progression insights
- **Unlimited profile edits:** Quick customization between events

**Pricing Model:**
- Monthly: $4.99/month (lower commitment, higher churn expected)
- Annual: $39.99/year (~67% discount incentive; ~$3.33/month effective)

**Financial Targets:**
- 5% volunteer conversion rate from Free tier
- 2-year average LTV: ~$80 (accounting for churn)
- Emphasis on annual plans for predictable cash flow

**Rationale:**
- Radar chart + analytics address volunteer pain points: "Where am I strong?" and "How do I improve?"
- Certificates enable resume/LinkedIn value, justifying subscription cost
- 30-day reassessment enables short feedback loops for committed volunteers
- Advanced shares amplify virality (LinkedIn integration = growth amplifier)

---

### 3. Organization Tier
**Target Users:** NGOs, event organizers, corporate volunteer coordinators, government agencies.

**Freemium Tier — Starter (Free)**
- List up to 2 events
- Search volunteer database (limited results: 50 per search)
- Basic analytics (total applications, event attendance)
- Volunteer profile browsing (public data only)
- No API access

**Purpose:** Reduce barrier to entry, generate org accounts for upsell.

---

**Growth Tier ($29/month or $299/year)**
- Unlimited event listings
- Full volunteer search + filtering (all 8 AURA competencies, AURA score threshold)
- Detailed analytics (conversion funnel, volunteer retention, event ROI)
- AURA API access (1,000 requests/month; ~$0.03 per API call @ cost)
- Volunteer request feature (send curated matches directly to volunteers)
- Attestation workflow (verify volunteer claims in public profile)
- Email campaign templates for event promotion
- Leaderboard creation for own events (drives volunteer engagement)

**Financial Targets:**
- 10% org adoption rate from Starter tier
- 18-month average LTV: ~$522 (1.5 years × $29 × 12 months)
- Primary revenue driver at scale

**Rationale:**
- API access attracts tech-forward orgs (tech nonprofits, government innovation labs)
- Attestation builds org-verified trust (orgs confirm volunteer skills)
- Leaderboards create competitive engagement (volunteers reassess to improve rank)
- Analytics justify ROI conversation with C-suite donors

---

**Enterprise Tier ($99/month or $999/year)**
- Dedicated API key (10,000 requests/month; can purchase add-ons at $0.01/call)
- Bulk volunteer matching (upload volunteer requirements, get recommendations)
- White-label AURA badges (org's logo on certificates)
- Custom assessment modules (org-specific competencies, weighted scoring)
- Priority support (Slack channel, 24-hour response SLA)
- Quarterly strategy reviews with Volaura team
- Advanced data export (CSV, integration webhooks)

**Target Users:** Large NGOs, government agencies, corporate volunteer programs (100+ volunteers/year).

**Financial Targets:**
- 2% org adoption rate from Growth tier
- 24-month LTV: ~$2,376 (2 years × $99 × 12)
- Premium support justifies price (reduces org onboarding overhead)

**Rationale:**
- White-label and custom modules create competitive moat (orgs don't switch)
- Bulk matching reduces org friction (automated vs manual filtering)
- API quotas scale with org needs (predictable revenue growth per customer)

---

### 4. Event Partnership (Revenue)
**Event:** Major strategic events in target regions (May 2026 onwards).

**Monetization:**
- **Sponsor partnerships:** Event branding + Volaura AURA integration (paid sponsorship)
- **Event-specific leaderboard:** Volunteers assessed during event compete on public leaderboard
- **Data insights report:** Post-event summary for event organizers (competency profile of attendees, volunteer engagement metrics)
- **Press coverage licensing:** Case study distribution rights to international volunteer platforms

**Financial Targets:**
- $10–20K sponsorship revenue per major event
- ~500 volunteers assessed per event week
- 20–30% conversion to Pro tier post-event (viral + credibility boost)

**Rationale:**
- Major events provide organic acquisition (no paid CAC for volunteers)
- Press coverage accelerates regional brand recognition
- Post-event momentum drives pro conversions and org partnerships

### 5. Future Revenue Streams (Post-Launch)

#### AURA API for Third-Party Platforms
- SaaS platforms serving volunteering, nonprofits, or CSR (corporate social responsibility)
- Pricing: $0.02–0.05 per API call (pay-per-use), tiers based on monthly volume
- Use cases: VolunteerHub integration, UN volunteer portal integration, corporate Slack bot

#### Premium Assessment Modules
- Industry-specific assessments (healthcare, education, emergency response, conservation)
- Pricing: $2–5 per assessment (org or individual buyer)
- Example: "Crisis Response Competency Assessment" for humanitarian orgs

#### Certification Partnerships
- Partner with internationally recognized bodies (UN, ISO, industry associations)
- Co-branded certificates (AURA score + partner verification)
- Premium pricing: $9.99 per certificate download
- Example: "Verified Field Leadership Certificate" bundled with Pro tier

#### Recruitment Agency Fees
- Premium organizations pay commission for vetted hires into permanent roles
- Model: 5–10% of hired candidate's annual salary (if applicable)

## Pricing Philosophy

### Principle 1: Free Tier Must Be Genuinely Useful
- A free tier that's crippled drives users to competitors
- Volaura's free tier delivers: complete AURA assessment + public profile + event discovery
- This is a **complete product**, not a "limited trial"
- Conversion to paid happens via *value*, not *restriction*

### Principle 2: Pro Tier Solves Real Pain Points
- Free tier: "What's my volunteer profile?"
- Pro tier: "How do I optimize my volunteering career?"
- Radar chart + analytics + certificates = professional credentialing narrative
- Pricing ($40/year) is 2–3 coffee subscriptions: low anchor for high perceived value

### Principle 3: Org Tier Reduces Friction for Orgs
- Freemium removes evaluation risk (zero commitment, full feature trial)
- Growth tier ($29/mo) is 1–2 staff hours saved per month via bulk matching + analytics
- Enterprise tier ($99/mo) is <1% of a full-time HR role's salary (massive ROI)

### Principle 4: Transparency on AURA Score
- AURA score is never "locked behind a paywall"
- Free users see their full score and badge
- Pro adds *context* (percentiles, trends) not the *score itself*
- Maintains integrity and trust in the AURA credential

## Growth Levers

### Organic Viral Growth
1. **Public profiles:** Volunteers share AURA cards on WhatsApp, Telegram, LinkedIn
2. **Referral incentives:** Invite a volunteer, both get 30-day Pro free trial
3. **Leaderboard virality:** Top 10 volunteers featured on homepage (ego/competitive drive)
4. **Email campaigns:** Monthly AURA score digest + peer benchmarks

### Major Event Acquisition
1. **Event partnership booth:** Free AURA assessment at major events (500+ volunteer acquisition)
2. **Post-event email campaigns:** Convert event participants to Pro tier (high intent)
3. **Press coverage:** Event case study → media mentions → organic search traffic

### Organization Inbound
1. **Content marketing:** Blog posts on "How to Hire Volunteers" (SEO + thought leadership)
2. **Partner integrations:** VolunteerHub, UN volunteer portal, CSR platforms
3. **Sales outreach:** Direct outreach to 500+ CIS/MENA NGOs and gov agencies

### Geographic Expansion
1. **CIS region:** Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan (similar nonprofit ecosystems)
2. **MENA region:** UAE, Saudi Arabia, Jordan (emerging volunteer credentialing markets)
3. **Localization:** i18n infrastructure enables <2 week per-country launch (RU, AR, EN)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Pro Conversion Rate
- **Mitigation:** A/B test Pro upgrade messaging (radar chart demo vs analytics preview)
- **Fallback:** Expand freemium feature set (e.g., 1 competency details per month)
- **Timeline:** Assess at month 3 post-launch

### Risk: Orgs Prefer Manual Search
- **Mitigation:** Onboard top 5 NGOs with white-glove setup; showcase ROI in case studies
- **Fallback:** Offer 3-month free Growth trial to early orgs
- **Timeline:** Assess at month 6

### Risk: AURA Score Ceiling (Too Many Gold/Platinum Badges)
- **Mitigation:** Implement decay (AURA scores reset to 50% every 2 years; reassessment required)
- **Fallback:** Introduce "Verified" badges (org attestation adds credibility weight)
- **Timeline:** Monitor at month 3

### Risk: Regulatory Scrutiny (Data Privacy, Assessment Bias)
- **Mitigation:** GDPR + local data residency; bias audits on LLM evaluations; transparency in AURA methodology
- **Fallback:** Third-party assessment audit (academic partnership)
- **Timeline:** Plan before CIS/MENA expansion
