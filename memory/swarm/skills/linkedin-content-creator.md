## Volaura's LinkedIn Strategy: 3 Layers

### Layer 1 — AURA Score Share (feature design)
When a user achieves a badge or improves a score, we give them a ready-to-post LinkedIn format:

```
Hook variant A (achievement):
"I just got verified in Communication & Leadership on Volaura.
Score: 87/100. Here's what the assessment actually tested."

Hook variant B (growth story):
"3 months ago my Leadership score was 52. Today: 71.
Here's the 1 thing that changed it."

Hook variant C (proof):
"LinkedIn says I have 'leadership skills.' Volaura has the score.
The difference: one is claimed, one is tested."
```

**Technical need:** Share button → pre-filled LinkedIn post text + score card image (OG image generator) + link back to profile.

### Layer 2 — Profile Optimization (coach feature)
AURA Coach (existing skill) should include LinkedIn profile recommendations:
- "Your Leadership score (71) is strong. Add it to your LinkedIn headline."
- Headline formula: `[Role] | Volaura Verified: [Top 2 competencies] | [Outcome]`
- Example: "Marketing Manager | Verified: Communication 87 + Leadership 71 | Helping brands grow in CIS"

### Layer 3 — Content Pillars for Professional Users
Volaura professionals need 3 content pillars:
1. **Verification stories** — "I took the AURA assessment. Here's what I learned about myself."
2. **Growth path** — "My weakest competency was X. Here's how I'm closing the gap."
3. **Social proof** — "An org found me through my AURA score. Here's how it works."

## Algorithm Rules for Volaura Content

- No external links in post body → "Link in comments" or first comment
- First line must earn the scroll — verification/score numbers work as hooks
- OG image with score badge → high dwell time (visual stops the scroll)
- 3-5 hashtags: `#careeradvice #Azerbaijan #verifiedskills #professionaldev #Volaura`
