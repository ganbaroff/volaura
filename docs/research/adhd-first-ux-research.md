## 26 Design Rules (implementable)

### ADHD-First UI Rules
1. One action per screen in onboarding + assessment
2. Floor mechanic trivially achievable (<2 min daily engagement)
3. State always saved — never lose progress on app close
4. No punishment mechanics — wrong answers = retry, never lose hearts
5. Never cover system clock (ADHD time-blindness)
6. Motion only on achievement — not hover, scroll, or decoration
7. Left-align all text — no centered/justified body text
8. Notification opt-out prominent — one tap in settings
9. Focus mode toggle — hide feed/social, show only personal progress

### Gamification Rules (positive reinforcement ONLY)
10. "Earn your next badge" NOT "Don't lose your streak"
11. Streak freeze earned through activity, never purchased
12. Leaderboards opt-in only, never default visible
13. Variable reward MAGNITUDE not EXISTENCE (always get something, sometimes more)
14. Rare achievement-only items — at least one tier unpurchasable
15. Crystal spending = "invest in visibility" NOT "spend crystals"
16. Daily crystal cap with diminishing returns
17. Burning mechanic — peer verification costs crystals (removes from circulation)

### Economy Rules
18. Monitor tap/sink ratio weekly — alert if taps > sinks by 15% for 7 days
19. No direct crystal purchase for money — earned-only supply
20. Provenance tracking — every crystal has source action (visible to orgs)
21. Achievement-locked items — non-purchasable, non-transferable

### CIS/AZ Market Rules
22. Design at 375px primary breakpoint (mid-range Android majority)
23. WebP, lazy load, <200KB initial JS bundle
24. Birbank + m10 integration BEFORE paid features
25. AZN primary currency display, USD secondary
26. AZ text 20-30% longer — test all components at AZ locale

## Key Research Sources
- Duolingo: 600+ streak experiments. Floor mechanic = 40% more users retained.
- SuperBetter (Jane McGonigal): user-defined goals, cooperative not competitive, no punishment
- EU banned TikTok infinite scroll (2025) — engagement without stopping points = regulatory liability
- Loss aversion is dopamine-modulated — ADHD brains LEAST equipped to handle it
- Peak Shift in UI: amplify achievement signals beyond natural proportion
- AZ digital: 9.23M online, 12.2M mobile connections, 45 Mbps avg, 91% electronic payments
- Payment: Birbank 3.4M users, m10 growing, Stripe NOT locally familiar
