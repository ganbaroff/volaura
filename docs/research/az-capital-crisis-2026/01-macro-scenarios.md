## 2. Four macro scenarios

### Scenario A — Peg holds through 2026, gradual glide after

**Probability: 55-65%** (Atlas draft, not AI consensus — consensus would say 75-80%; I'm discounting because consensus missed 2015)

**Narrative:**
- Oil remains $70-90 range. War brings net oil windfall even if spiky.
- CBAR + SOFAZ reserves continue building or stable at $80-90B.
- CBAR intervenes at the edges to keep 1.7000. Cheap to do when net oil $ flowing in.
- Inflation stays 5-6%. Rate corridor tools sufficient.
- BTC pipeline survives. Iran-AZ de-escalation holds at local level even if Iran war drags on.
- Peg weakens marginally after 2027 (S&P/Fitch view) to 1.75-1.80 by 2028.

**Triggers that keep us in A:**
- Brent stays >$60
- Reserves grow or flat
- Black market FX premium stays <1%
- No BTC pipeline physical damage
- Aliyev-Pezeshkian diplomatic line holds

**Asset implications (brief):** Cash AZN fine. Gold moves with global flows, not AZ-specific. Real estate appreciates nominal 7-10%. USD cash +0% real (peg holds). CNY irrelevant.

---

### Scenario B — Soft devaluation 5-15% through end-2027

**Probability: 20-25%**

**Narrative:**
- Oil declines to $60-70 range by late 2026 / 2027 as war de-escalates (EIA path).
- SOFAZ drawdown accelerates beyond projected $70B → $64.8B.
- Non-oil growth sluggish. Inflation sticky 5-7%.
- CBAR allows controlled slide: 1.70 → 1.75 → 1.80 through 2027.
- No crisis, no capital controls. Orderly.
- Mirrors Fitch/ING post-2027 consensus.

**Triggers that move us to B:**
- Brent drops $60-70 for 6+ months
- SOFAZ drawdown >$8B in a quarter
- Fitch/Moody's downgrade outlook from positive/stable to negative
- CBAR rate hikes
- Public CBAR language shifts from "stable" to "managed"

**Asset implications:** USD cash +5-15% in AZN terms. Gold neutral (already priced). Real estate -3% to -5% real. AZN cash -5-15% real. AZN deposits at 15% rate beat mild devaluation.

---

### Scenario C — Hard peg break 25-50% (CEO's fear scenario)

**Probability: 8-12%** on a 12-month horizon. Higher (15-20%) on 24-month.

**Narrative (what would actually trigger this):**
- BTC pipeline physically attacked, oil exports disrupted 30-60 days
- OR Iran war escalates drawing AZ in directly → infrastructure bombed
- OR oil crashes to $40s and stays (unlikely near-term but possible 2027 in recession scenario)
- OR regional bank run triggered by Armenia conflict reignition
- CBAR burns $15-25B defending. Fails by Month 3-4.
- Free float. 30-50% devaluation overnight.
- Capital controls within 30 days (2016 playbook).
- USD-loan defaults spike. Banking stress.

**Speed:** Historical 2015 — $8B burned over 11 months before float. This time CBAR has more rope ($85B), but faster attack would compress timeline.

**Probability reasoning:**
- Reserves 6.5x stronger than 2015. This alone drops base probability.
- Oil war is net-positive for AZ ex-infrastructure hit. Fiscal surplus protects.
- BUT: war expansion, BTC hit, oil crash — ALL three tail risks exist simultaneously for first time since 2020.

**Triggers that move us toward C (in order of leading → lagging):**
1. BTC pipeline physical damage — immediate
2. Black market FX premium opens above 5% — days/weeks warning
3. Exchange booths run out of USD — days warning
4. Deposit withdrawal queues at AZ banks — hours
5. CBAR reserve drop >$2B in a week — days
6. CBAR tightens currency regulation language — weeks warning
7. Rating agencies put AZ on watch negative — weeks warning

**Asset implications:** USD cash +30-50% in AZN. Gold +40-60% (war premium + devaluation compound). AZN cash -30-50% real. AZN deposits -15-25% (some rate compensation). Real estate -20-40% real (if sold in first year post-crisis). **USD-linked debt becomes POISON. AZN-denominated debt becomes CHEAPER.**

---

### Scenario D — Stagflation / slow rot, peg holds nominally

**Probability: 12-17%**

**Narrative:**
- Peg defended stubbornly. No devaluation.
- But growth stalls to 0.5-1.5%. Inflation stuck 6-8%. Real wages decline.
- Reserves drain slowly. Non-oil sector doesn't compensate for oil decline.
- 2028-2030 looks worse than 2026.
- No "event" — death by a thousand cuts.

**Triggers:** Oil $55-70 sustained. Non-oil growth <2%. Youth unemployment rises. Remittances from Russia decline. FDI stalls.

**Asset implications:** AZN cash -6-8% real/year. Gold real positive. RE flat real. USD neutral (peg holds). Best hedge is real assets + diversification.

| Scenario | 12-mo probability | 24-mo probability | Key indicator shift |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| A — Peg holds | 55-65% | 45-55% | Oil >$70, reserves stable/up |
| B — Soft devaluation 5-15% | 20-25% | 25-30% | Oil $60-70, slow SOFAZ drain |
| C — Hard break 25-50% | 8-12% | 15-20% | BTC attack, oil <$50, war escalation |
| D — Stagflation no break | 12-17% | 10-15% | Oil $55-70 + non-oil stall |

Expected outcome weighted by probability (12-mo):
- 60% × 0% + 22% × -10% + 10% × -35% + 13% × 0% = **-5.7% real depreciation expected**

This is the "blended" outcome. Actual outcomes cluster — either ~0 or big. Planning for the mode, not the mean.

## 4. Critical self-review

### What I might be getting wrong

1. **Consensus anchoring.** S&P, Fitch, IMF all say stable. Consensus also said stable in Nov 2015, one month before float. I am discounting this (put Scenario A at 55-65% vs consensus 75-80%) but may not discount enough.

2. **Reserves are strong — but composition matters.** $64.8B SOFAZ is **35% gold + 25% equities + 10% real estate**. That's a sovereign portfolio that can't be instant-liquidated without cost. Liquid USD portion of combined reserves is probably $25-35B, not $85B. If I'm wrong on composition, real defensive dry powder is lower than headline.

3. **Rating agencies upgrade near the top.** Fitch BBB- upgrade now = historically a reverse-indicator. Fitch upgraded Turkey to BBB- in 2013 before the lira crisis. Pattern isn't destiny but rhymes.

4. **SOFAZ itself is 35% gold.** The country is hedging itself. If sovereign doesn't trust fiat — why should retail?

5. **BTC pipeline probability could be higher than 8-12%.** Iran named it as target. One attack plot already foiled. Pattern of Iranian asymmetric retaliation says more attempts likely. Probability drift upward if war drags past 6 months.

6. **Public data lags 1-3 months.** If reserves are draining in April, CBAR publishes in May/June. Atlas sees nothing until then. CEO's on-ground signals are leading indicators I can't source.

7. **"Intuition says crisis in 2-3 weeks" is likely wrong on timing but could be right on direction 6-18 months out.** I may be too quick to say "CEO wrong." CEO may be right-eventually but early-now.

8. **Oil windfall is real only if oil stays up.** EIA forecasts declining to $76 avg 2027. If ceasefire happens and oil crashes to $55, AZ loses windfall AND faces fiscal stress. This is the most under-analyzed scenario.

9. **Political shock risk (Armenia-AZ peace collapse, SOCAR leadership change, Aliyev succession tension) is off my model entirely.** These are high-impact, unforecastable shocks.

10. **Wikipedia / oilprice.com / specialeurasia.com are not peer-reviewed macro analysis.** Some of my geopolitical facts come from aggregator sources. Higher-confidence on IMF, CBAR, S&P. Lower-confidence on the breaking-news pieces.

### What I think I'm getting right

- Pre-2015 crisis reserves vs today is the single strongest peg-defense signal. 6.5x cushion is real.
- External debt <10% GDP removes one classic EM crisis trigger.
- Fiscal surplus 8% GDP 2023 removes another.
- Liberal FX regime means controls would be a **deliberate** policy action, not organic. Signals available in advance.
- 2015 playbook is known: burn reserves → float → controls 30 days later. Monitorable sequence.

## 5. What this means before Layers 2-6

- **Base case: No crisis in next 2-3 weeks.** Probability of hard break in that window is <3%.
- **12-month probability of any-flavor depreciation (B or C): ~30-35%.** Real and meaningful.
- **24-month probability of any-flavor depreciation: ~40-50%.** Preparation justified.
- **Tail risk (BTC attack or oil crash) is live.** Monitor.
- **CEO's intuition is directionally plausible on 18-36 month horizon, very unlikely on 2-3 week horizon.**

This conclusion feeds Layers 2-6 but does NOT pre-commit to any asset allocation. Layer 6 will produce the allocation via the engine.
